Fried Eggs

Description:

A scrumptious recipe for the perfect fried egg!

Ingredients:
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
Steps:
  1. Preheat small stainless steel skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add oil or butter to the pan, swirl around for a few seconds, then drop in whole egg.
  3. Season egg with a sprinkle of salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Cook until egg whites are set and the edges are stating to get brown and crispy (adjust heat as necessary).
  5. Remove egg from pan and serve immediately.
