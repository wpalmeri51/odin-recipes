Grilled Chicken

Description

A scrumptious recipe for the perfect grilled chicken!

Ingredients:
  • 1 boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp light olive oil, canola, or vegetable oil
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
Steps:
  1. Preheat grill over highest heat for at least 10 minutes.
  2. While grill is preheating, remove chicken from fridge, coat with oil, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Once grill is hot, clean the grates (if necessary) and using tongs, place chicken breast flattest side down directly on grill.
  4. Allow to cook for several minutes on one side until chicken gets good color and grill marks, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until internal temperature reads 145 F at chicken's thickest point.
  6. Remove from grill, allow to rest for a few minutes, then serve.
